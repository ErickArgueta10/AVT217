Portfolio Website Design Brief


Problem Statement

Design a website to effectively display my portfolio pieces in a digital matter.

Problem Description

The website will serve as a representation tool. This portfolio website will include a place to display all of my portfolio pieces and should include at least 10 works with descriptions and my resume. This is site will display an HTML version of my resume as well as a downloadable PDF. There should also be clear navigation throughout.The pictures should be high quality and saved in a format that would reduce large file sizes. Good typography and composition choices should be incorporated into the website. Lastly, the portfolio should include my contact information. The entire website should be in HTML form.

Target Audience

The target audience is for graphic designers, other art professionals/students, or potential employers. I would also like my portfolio website to be visually appealing and exemplify my design potential through my pieces.

Project Goals

I want the design to represent me visually and be a clean, professional design. My intent is that when professionals view my work, they will want to hire me. I also want my work to give me a good reputation and inspire others with my work

Constraints

I only have a a minimal amount of knowledge for coding. One of the constraints I can think of is the amount of visual appeal I can add to my website. However, it is another one of my goals to push myself and produce a website that I am proud of.

Budget

The budget for this website will be very minimal.

Deliverables

The only deliverable for this project is a well-designed, well-organized portfolio website.
