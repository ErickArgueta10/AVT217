TSC IS NOT ME

TSC is not ME is a book that I wrote and designed on a rare, genetic disorder called tuberous sclerosis. The book is separated in two sections: one section is research-based while the other tells the story of my life, living with tuberous sclerosis from the perspectives of my mother and myself.
